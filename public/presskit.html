PRESS TEXT

Joe & The Anchor is a flourishing pop band from Malmö, blending a
modern pop sensibility with indie influences that tugs at your heartstrings.
If you’ve been looking for Sweden’s answer to bands like LANY and

The 1975, look no further.

Since dropping their debut EP “Antidote” in 2019 via Sony Music, the band has
been on an upwards trajectory and developed their music into a strong and
cohesive live act. With years of live experience, JATA has already performed
on big stages and festivals around Sweden and Europe – including as
support act for one of Sweden’s biggest rock bands Mando Diao.
During the pandemic the band has been making waves online, with their
cover of The 1975’s song ‘If You’re Too Shy (Let Me Know)’ quickly reaching
60K views through organic spread. JATA has in a short amount of time
acquired a very engaged international fan base who calls themselves

Sailors, eagerly waiting for the band’s next move.

The band is currently building up to their debut album ‘Coming Of Age’
being released later this spring, and the singles 'California' and 'Leaving You
Now' confirm that the band is setting the tone for the future of Scandinavian
pop. With a distinct visual style and a captivating stage presence,
Joe & The Anchor always deliver unforgettable evenings.
